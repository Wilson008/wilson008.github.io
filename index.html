About Me

About Me

I have worked in the industry for seven years, four years as a software engineer developing software for rail transit control systems, and the other three years as a project manager managing all the development of ultrasonic medical diagnostic systems for a medical start-up company.

After working for seven years, I started my PhD in the Software Engineering Division of the Chalmers | University of Gothenburg in Sweden. My research field is model-driven software engineering (MDSE), especially the domain modeling language (DSL) part. Additionally, because of my former work experience, I am particularly interested in software project management, especially the project management of safety-critical system development.
